Related packages

wilson

A package for the running and matching of Wilson coefficients above and below the electroweak scale.

Forms the basis of flavio's handling of dimension-6 Wilson coefficients beyond the Standard Model.

wilson-eft.github.io

smelli

A package providing a global likelihood function in the space of dimension-six Wilson coefficients in the Standard Model Effective Field Theory (SMEFT).

High-level wrapper around flavio providing a curated, self-consistent, and user-friendly global likelihood and convenience methods.

smelli.github.io
